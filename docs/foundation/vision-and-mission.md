# Vision & Mission

## Vision

Every Ethiopian citizen, in every region and in their own language,
experiences government digital services that are simple, accessible,
trustworthy, and consistent — regardless of which ministry, agency,
regional government, municipality, or institution built them.

## Mission

ETHDS provides the shared design language, components, patterns,
templates, and standards that let Ethiopian government teams build
digital services faster, more consistently, and to a higher bar of
accessibility and usability than any one team could achieve alone — by
giving them production-ready building blocks instead of asking them to
start from a blank page.

## Who ETHDS Is For

- **National Government** — federal ministries and agencies
- **Regional Governments** — regional state digital services
- **Municipal Governments** — city and woreda-level services
- **Public Agencies** — independent government bodies
- **Educational Institutions** — public universities and exam/credential bodies
- **Public Service Portals** — citizen-facing service platforms

## Why This Matters

Without a shared design system, every government digital project
re-solves the same problems — accessibility, multilingual support,
low-bandwidth performance, consistent navigation — independently, at
different quality levels, with different outcomes for citizens. ETHDS
exists so that solving these problems once, well, and openly benefits
every team that builds on it.

This vision is grounded in the [Core Principles](design-philosophy.md)
