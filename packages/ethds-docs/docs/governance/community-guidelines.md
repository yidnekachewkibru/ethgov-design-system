# Community Guidelines

How the ETHDS community works together. These guidelines complement the
[Code of Conduct](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
(which sets the floor for behaviour) with practical norms for
collaborating well.

## Where things happen

- **GitHub Issues** — bugs and concrete feature requests.
- **GitHub Discussions** — open-ended questions and early proposals before
  they become an [RFC](rfc-process.md).
- **Pull Requests** — code, docs, and translation review.
- **RFCs**
  ([`packages/ethds-rfcs/`](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/tree/main/packages/ethds-rfcs))
  — significant design/architecture decisions.

(A dedicated public chat/forum will be added once the community outgrows
GitHub-native tools — see the [community model](community-model.md).)

## Working norms

- **Assume good faith.** Contributors span many languages, time zones, and
  levels of experience. Be patient, especially with non-native English or
  first-time contributors.
- **Start with citizen needs.** Frame proposals and reviews around what
  helps citizens, not preferences or internal convenience.
- **Discuss in the open.** Decisions and their reasoning are public
  ([Open by Default](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/blob/main/docs/design-principles/10-open-by-default.md));
  avoid side-channel decisions.
- **Be specific and kind in review.** Tie feedback to standards (the
  checklists, WCAG criteria) and suggest a path forward, not just "no".
- **Prefer reuse.** Check existing components/patterns before proposing new
  ones
  ([Reuse Before Building](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/blob/main/docs/design-principles/07-reuse-before-building.md)).

## Language

- English is the working language for issues/PRs to keep discussion
  accessible to the widest contributor base; Amharic is welcome too.
- Translation contributions are made in the target language and reviewed by
  that language's reviewer.

## Getting help

- New contributor? Start at
  [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
- Unsure if something needs an RFC? Ask in Discussions.
- Found an accessibility barrier or security issue? See the
  [accessibility framework](/docs/accessibility) /
  [SECURITY.md](https://github.com/yidnekachewkibru/ethgov-design-system/blob/main/SECURITY.md).

## Recognition

Contributions of every kind — code, translation, accessibility testing,
docs, triage, support — are valued and count toward the
[contributor ladder](contributor-ladder.md). A healthy, welcoming
community is itself a project goal.
