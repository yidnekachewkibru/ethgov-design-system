First publishable release of `@ethds/templates`: tested React compositions for all 12 documented page templates (National Portal, Ministry, and Agency homepages; Service Landing, Service Application, Search Results, News listing + article, and Contact pages; Citizen Dashboard; 404, 403, and 500 error pages). The Service Application Page hosts the `@ethds/patterns` Application Submission flow, which becomes a real dependency of this package.
